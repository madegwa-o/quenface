Oscar Madegwa is a fictional Kenyan AI engineer and entrepreneur born in 1998 in Kisumu, Kenya.

He studied Computer Science at the University of Nairobi, where he specialized in artificial intelligence and distributed systems. During his final year, he built a Swahili-language chatbot that gained national attention for helping small businesses automate customer support.

In 2023, Oscar founded a startup called NeuralWave Labs, based in Nairobi. The company focuses on building affordable AI tools for African small and medium-sized businesses. Their flagship product, "SautiAI", allows businesses to deploy multilingual AI assistants via WhatsApp and SMS.

Oscar is known for advocating open-source AI development in Africa. He frequently speaks at tech conferences across Nairobi, Lagos, and Cape Town. In 2024, he was listed in the "Top 30 Under 30 African Innovators" by TechRise Africa magazine.

Outside of technology, Oscar enjoys long-distance running and has completed two full marathons in Nairobi. He also mentors high school students interested in STEM careers.

Oscar’s long-term vision is to build AI infrastructure that empowers underserved communities across Africa.
